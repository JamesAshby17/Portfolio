About Me

Hey! I am an incoming student-athlete at Carnegie Mellon University, where I will play soccer while studying Electrical and Computer Engineering. Beleive it or not, I developed a passion for programming through my love for soccer. You see, I wanted some way of predicting the premier league (and the chances of my beloved Liverpool winning). I was enrolled in a Computer Science course at school, and I tried my hand at building this algorithm with what I had learned and what I could find online. In the process of designing this PL simulator, I fell in love with the process of critical thinking, problem solving, and the satisfaction of seeing the program come together!

Projects

  • Project 1: Premier League simulator (Java)

    Developed a simulator for a premier league season based on the current data of xG, xGA, each team's Home/Away form (and of course luck!). Using the relevant data of past seasons and comparing the simulation's results with the actual results, the likelihood for my algorithm to predict the correct place of any given team was 17%

  • Project 2: Order Processor (Java)

    Developed a program in java and website interface using HTML to create an online storefront and process orders for Items from the shop
